Project Overview

Wanted Curve Bullet is a physics-based shooter where players control bullet trajectory in real-time, bending them around corners and obstacles to eliminate targets. Inspired by the iconic curved bullet scenes from the movie "Wanted," the game challenges players to master precise trajectory control with satisfying slow-motion kill cams.

My Contributions

🎯

Bullet Curving Mechanic

Designed and implemented the core curving system using bezier curves and real-time player input for trajectory control.

⚙️

Physics System

Custom physics for bullet flight, collision detection, and ricochet mechanics with satisfying impact feedback.

🗺️

Level Design

Created increasingly complex levels that teach players the curving mechanic through environmental puzzles and obstacles.

🎨

Game Feel & Juice

Trail renderers, slow-motion kill cam, screen shake, and particle effects on impact for satisfying game feel.
